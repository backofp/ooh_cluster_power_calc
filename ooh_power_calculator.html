Methodology: Analysis unit is the precinct (clustered within metro). ZIP-level effect = precinct effect × precincts per ZIP. Expected SE scales from the comparable experiment anchor as SE_new = SE_anchor × precincts_per_ZIP × √(n_comp_eff / total_eff_metros). Effective metros weight each grand stratum by variance inflation 1/(p×(1−p)), where p is the treatment propensity score. Grand stratum 4 (pscore=0.25) contributes the least power per ZIP. Comparable experiment: education OOH experiment, net_dem_votes_2way outcome, effect=324, SE=193, p=0.093, CR2 cluster-robust SEs.
